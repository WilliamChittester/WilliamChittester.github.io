About Me

Current Work:

Cambria County

I am a Geographic Information Systems (GIS) Technician with Cambria County, in central Pennsylvania. I maintain the county surface and mineral tax parcel maps, develop public ArcGIS WebApps and internal ArcGIS Apps and Dashboards. As well as, create paper and PDF maps for Attorneys, Surveyors, Title Abstractors, and the General Public and provide technical guidance and recommendations on mapping and deed-related questions and issues.

I have also assisted with research and development of the Cambria County indoor mapping program through, program testing, mission planning and the collection of data using drones and mobile Lidar.

Freelance Cartography and Geospatial Anaylsis

As a freelance cartographer I have designed and published custom maps and provided GIS analysis using QGIS and Adobe Illustrator for clients such as:

  • PA Bureau of Forestry District 6
  • Penn State University Transportation Services
  • Custaloga Town Scout Reservation
  • Dauntless Fire Company

Work

Cambria County:

Mining and Mineral Ownership WebApp

This application was built using ESRI WebApp tools and MapViewer, and displays current mineral parcel ownership information within Cambria County and also shows active, planned, and past mining activity throughout PA using data obtained from PA DEP.

Building Permit Dashboard

The Building Permit Dashboard was developed to help tax assessors interpret the status of Cambria County’s Building Permit Database. The Dashboard includes a map of the building permits, can summarize the permits by type and the permit review status, and can be filtered by permit date, type, or municipality.
